About Me

Engineer in Naval Energy Systems and Digital Innovation

I'm an engineer and researcher passionate about sustainable maritime systems. I specialize in modeling, optimization, and control of hybrid power architectures, including PEBB-based converters, batteries, fuel cells, and diesel generators.

I collaborate with Elettra APS and the University of Genoa to design and test zero-emission vessels, leveraging digital twins, GAMS-based optimization, and MATLAB/Simulink simulations.

My mission is to bridge academic research and real-world impact for smart ports and clean navigation.
